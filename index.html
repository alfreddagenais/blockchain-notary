What we do

What we do is that we provide a unique proof of any electronic document.
On the Blockchain.

How it works


Blockchain Notary is an open source service that computes the cryptographic hash for your documents and store this cryptographic information on the Blockchain.

We use the SHA-2 algorithm to compute hash for your files and we never get to actually upload your file anywhere to a server.
This means that your document content is 100% secure and cryptographically protected along the way.

Using this service, you will receive the ultimate proof of your digital document yet it's secure and nobody but you gets to access the true content.
It's all actually quite simple and down to math and how Blockchain works. Blockchain rules. :)
